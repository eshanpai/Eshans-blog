**Overview:**
Though primarily focused on China, Meituan's model has shown immense promise. It’s a super app for everything from food delivery to hotel bookings, and its growth is due to the breadth of its offerings.

**Key Player: Meituan**
•	Focus: E-commerce, food delivery, and lifestyle services.
•	Growth: Over 500 million users in China.
•	Innovation: Meituan expanded into financial services, AI-powered logistics, and cloud computing.

**What’s Next?**
Expect Meituan to expand beyond China’s borders and dominate in the food tech and logistics space in Southeast Asia and India.
