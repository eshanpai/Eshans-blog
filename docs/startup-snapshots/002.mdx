**Overview**:
Upstox is one of India’s leading online discount brokerage platforms, making stock trading accessible to the masses. It offers low brokerage charges and an intuitive app that democratizes the stock market for new investors.
Key Player: Upstox
•	Focus: Discount brokerage services for retail investors.
•	Growth: Over 10 million customers, with rapid adoption in Tier 2 and 3 cities.
•	Innovation: Easy-to-use mobile app and low-cost investment options for equity, commodities, and mutual funds.

**What’s Next**?
With the rise of digital finance, Upstox will likely leverage AI and machine learning to provide personalized investment strategies, further simplifying stock trading for the average investor.
