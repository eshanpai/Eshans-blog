**Overview:**
Airwallex is an Australian fintech startup focused on providing global payment solutions. It allows businesses to manage their international payments, currency exchange, and financial services through one platform.

**Key Player: Airwallex**
•	Focus: Cross-border payments and financial infrastructure for businesses.
•	Growth: Raised over $300M in funding, becoming a unicorn in a short time.
•	Innovation: Provides APIs for businesses to handle global payments, simplifying currency exchange and cross-border transactions.

**What’s Next?**
Airwallex is poised to expand into new international markets, focus on increasing banking-as-a-service offerings, and provide integrated solutions for e-commerce businesses.
