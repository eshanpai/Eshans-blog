**Overview:**
Xero is a cloud-based accounting software solution for small businesses. Based in New Zealand, it simplifies financial management, from invoicing to tax returns, making accounting accessible to non-finance professionals.

**Key Player: Xero**
•	Focus: Cloud-based accounting software for SMBs.
•	Growth: Over 2 million customers globally.
•	Innovation: Xero’s ability to integrate with other apps and provide live data to businesses has been key to its success.

**What’s Next?**
Xero will likely continue expanding its global presence and integrate more automation and AI tools into the accounting process to serve growing small business needs.
