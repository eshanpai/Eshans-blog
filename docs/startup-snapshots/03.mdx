**Overview**:
Meesho is India’s largest social commerce platform, empowering millions of small businesses and resellers to sell products directly through social media channels. Meesho leverages the power of social media and word-of-mouth marketing to drive sales.
Key Player: Meesho
•	Focus: Social commerce and reseller network.
•	Growth: Over 10 million resellers and a customer base across Tier 1, 2, and 3 cities.
•	Innovation: Meesho enables individuals to run their own businesses with zero investment, providing access to a catalog of products from thousands of suppliers.

**What’s Next?**
Meesho’s future lies in expanding into international markets, providing local entrepreneurs with access to a broader range of products and markets. The potential to offer logistics and payment solutions will be a key driver of growth.
