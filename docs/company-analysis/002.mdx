🧠 **Business Model (Expanded)**
Reliance Industries has diversified into multiple industries with four major segments:
•	Petrochemicals & Refining: Original core business
•	Retail: India’s largest retailer by revenue
•	Telecom (Jio): Hypergrowth engine
•	Digital Services (Jio Platforms): Tech-driven platform with global partnerships

💰 **Financials (FY22–23)**
•	Revenue: ₹9.76 lakh crore (~USD $120B)
•	Net Profit: ₹74,000 crore (~USD $9B)
•	Debt: High, but actively being managed

🧩 **Strategy (Expanded)**
•	Strengths: Value chain integration, high capital access, large-scale bets on infrastructure
•	Risks: Complex conglomerate structure, regulatory scrutiny, execution risks

🧠 **Founder POV**
Reliance doesn’t just build businesses; it builds entire ecosystems.

⚖️ **Takeaway**
Empire-building works if done with timing and control.
