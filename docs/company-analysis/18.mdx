26 February 2025

🧠 **Business Model (Expanded)**
Shopify powers millions of e-commerce stores worldwide. It offers:
•	Subscriptions (Shopify Basic, Plus)
•	Transaction fees (Shopify Payments)
•	Apps, Themes, POS systems, Fulfillment Network

💰 **Financials (FY24)**
•	Revenue: $8.8B
•	Net Income: $700M
•	GMV: $235B

🧩 **Strategy (Expanded)**
•	Going “headless” (modular APIs for developers)
•	Big retail push with POS + Shop Pay
•	Divested logistics to reduce burn, refocused on core SaaS

⚠️ **Risks**
•	Amazon competition (Buy with Prime)
•	Lower AOV and churn from micro-sellers
•	Growing margin pressure post-COVID boom

🧠 **Founder POV**
Shopify gave small brands a voice. Now it’s helping them act global.

⚖️ **Takeaway**
The platform era isn’t over — but it needs focus, not bloat.
