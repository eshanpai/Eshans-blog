As a startup founder, you’re bombarded with opportunities, offers, and partnerships. Early on, I was saying “yes” to everything. But over time, I learned that saying “no” is one of the most powerful skills you can develop.



<h1>Focusing on Core Vision</h1>
It’s easy to chase shiny objects, but the real growth happens when you stay focused on what matters. There’s a temptation to try everything, but knowing when to reject opportunities — even the good ones — has been key to maintaining focus.
