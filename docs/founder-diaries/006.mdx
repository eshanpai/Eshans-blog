In the early days, I was all about scaling fast — and I thought that meant spending a lot. But then I realized that the best way to scale isn’t by spending big money. It's by being lean and smart with what you have.



<h1>Resourcefulness</h1>
There were moments where I had to get creative. From finding cost-effective marketing solutions to partnering with other small businesses, I realized that a lean mindset was the key to achieving more with less. Scaling isn’t about having a bigger budget; it’s about making every penny work harder.
