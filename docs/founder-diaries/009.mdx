Pivoting is one of the toughest decisions a founder has to make. When things aren’t working, the temptation is to stick with your original plan and hope things turn around. But sometimes, pivoting is the right answer.



<h1>Adapting to Change</h1>
I’ve had to pivot a few times. The market shifted, and our initial idea didn’t have the traction we expected. It was tough, but learning to adapt quickly and be open to change has been a defining moment for us. The lesson? Always listen to your market.
