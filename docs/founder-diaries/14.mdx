<h1>Mastering Focus</h1>
It’s easy to get distracted by shiny new ideas or urgent requests. But I’ve learned that focus is the key to success. The real challenge is saying no to the things that don't align with your vision, even if they seem tempting. It’s about keeping your eyes on the prize, even when there’s noise.
